How I Discovered Daniel Fast

I have to think very hard in other to find something that I like about my job. It is a long and complicated story. I have been with them since 2001. For the past twelve months, I have been praying a lot for some kind of change. Last October, I was talking about it with one of nieces. We sometimes share inspirational video and text messages on WhatsApp. That day she mentioned a book titled The Circle Maker by Mike Batterson, pastor of the Universal Church based in Washington DC. The author:"shares powerful insights from the true legend of Honi the Circle Maker, a first-century Jewish sage whose bold prayer ended a drought and saved a generation".

I bought the Kindle version of the book on Amazon. In chapter 14, page 163, there is a section titled "Daniel Fast" in reference to the Bible verse: "I ate no choice food; no meat or wine touched my lips; and I used no lotions at all until the three weeks were over" (Daniel 10:3). I fasted for ten days. Did it work spiritually? Nope. But it was a wonder to my body. It was unlightened. Unfortunately, it ended on the Wednesday before Thanksgiving. I had to cook for family members. It was very difficult not to eat all those foods. However, I did a lighter version for the first three weeks of December. It was stunning. I felt like my body was free of any toxin or impurity. Here is how I did it.

My Daniel Fast Version

Not knowing exactly how to do the fast, I googled it and found plenty of books and info. The basic is avoiding certain foods and favor some others.

Important: Try not to stay hungry all day long to avoid temptation.

  • I.- Foods To Avoid And Eat:


    • Foods to Avoid
    • Sugar
    • Meats
    • Coffee
    • Dairy Products
    • Juice
    • Breads, pasta, flour, crackers (unless made from sprouted ancient grains)
    • Oils
    • Processed foods in any forms
      • Foods to Eat
    • Vegetables
    • Whole Grains
    • Beans
    • Natural unprocessed spices
    • Nuts
    • Coconut oil and milk from fresh grated coconut
    • Homemade Foods with unprocessed ingredients

  • II.- Meals

    • Lemon or salted hot water first thing in the morning.

    • Hot chocolate made with cacao, coconut milk and spices ( cinnamon, star anise, lemon or lime skin)

      Or

      Smoothie made Fruit + milked or grated coconut or water + oat + yeast germ

    • Coconut water from young coconuts. The young ones have more water. The water fills you up for about two hours. Or Fresh fruit soaked in hot water
    • Unsalted nuts to avoid extra ingredients in the fancy ones .
    • As dinner, have kabocha squash soup or tomato soup or other soup, as long as, processed ingredients are kept away
